Our Story

We have instruments like keyboard , guitar, melodica, clap-box. We have great experienced singers , who love to perform their talents in a very unique manner !
We hosted our first music show at Tharang 23 , which is the tech fest arranged by Jyothi Engineering College Cheruthuruthy. We were very happy that many of our friends supported on conducting that event and made it into a huge success!
